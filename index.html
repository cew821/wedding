Our adventure began in 1983...
Jess was born on Aug 1, 1983 in Kentucky. Shortly after she moved to Boston with her family
Three weeks later, Charles was born in Minneapolis
18 years later, Jess graduated high school in Boston and left for Washington D.C. to attend George Washington University. There, she met Charles' cousin Chris
A year later, Charles finished high school and packed his bags for Cambridge, MA to attend college at Harvard
Jess graduated college, and spent a few years working before moving to Tanzania to attend grad school
Meanwhile, Charles stayed in Boston where he worked as a technology consultant
Jess returned from Africa and moved to California, where she attended law school at UC Irvine
In 2010, Jess returned to Boston for an internship.

On a warm summer night, Charles hosted a party in Boston while his cousin Chris was in town. Chris invited some college friends to the party. One of those friends was Jess.

Charles served her a Dark & Stormy, and she was sold.

They have been together ever since.

Our Engagement

May 12, 2013 was a sunny spring day, so we decided to pack some sandwiches and bike to Great Falls for a picnic. Charles offered to pack the lunches and carry the bag, and he slipped in some extra cargo while Jess wasn't looking.


At the falls we found a quiet rock by the water to eat our PB&Js. As we watched the waterfall, Jess told Charles how much she loved their adventures. Suddenly, Jess realized Charles was down on one knee with a ring in his hand. He told her he was excited for a lifetime of adventures together, and asked if she would like to marry him...

She said yes!

About the Weekend

Hotels

We have held rooms at the Garrison Inn and the Essex Street Inn for out of town guests. Both inns are located in downtown Newburyport, a seaside town that is one of our favorite places in Massachussets. Newburyport is about an hour north of Boston.

To book, contact the Garrison Inn at 978-499-8500 or the Essex Street Inn at 978-465-3148 and ask for the Worthington/Glynn block.

Please note, the dates will appear sold out in the hotels' online systems. You must call to book a room in our block.

Transportation

Transportation from Newburyport to the ceremony and reception venue will be provided to and from the Garrison Inn in Newburyport. Buses will depart promptly at 4:15pm.

For guests wishing to drive, there is ample parking at Moraine Farm.
